[h1]GTI Weapon Wear[/h1]

Weapons in vanilla RimWorld never wear out from use, a pistol you have fired for ten years is as good as new. This mod changes that: [b]weapons slowly take damage as you use them[/b], and it adds easy ways to keep all of your gear repaired so a worn weapon is never a dead end.

[h1]Weapons wear out as you use them[/h1]
[list]
[*]Every shot fired and every melee swing has a [b]small chance[/b] to knock a point of condition off the weapon. Most uses do nothing, it adds up gradually over a lot of fighting, not all at once.
[*][b]Better quality weapons last longer.[/b] A legendary rifle wears far slower than an awful one.
[*]A weapon [b]will not destroy itself[/b] from normal use. Once it is worn down to almost nothing, your pawn stops using it so you get a chance to fix it. A weapon is only truly lost if it is destroyed some other way (like an explosion).
[/list]

[h1]Repair your gear[/h1]
Add the matching repair bill and your crafters will fix damaged items at the bench that fits them:
[list]
[*][b]Weapons[/b] at the machining table
[*][b]Armor[/b] at the smithy
[*][b]Clothing[/b] at the tailoring bench
[*][b]Utility gear[/b] (shield belts, packs, etc.) at the fabrication bench
[/list]
Repairs happen [b]gradually[/b] and the item keeps its [b]exact quality, material, and name[/b], you are fixing the real item, not replacing it. A repair costs a fraction of what the item was originally made from (steel for a steel gun, cloth for a shirt, and so on), and the more damaged it is, the more it costs. Components are never needed.

[h1]Automatic weapon upkeep (no micromanagement)[/h1]
Just like colonists automatically replace worn-out clothes, your pawns will [b]automatically repair the weapon they are carrying[/b] when it drops below a condition you choose.
[list]
[*]There is [b]nothing to enable per pawn[/b] and [b]no work type is required[/b], it just works in their spare time and never pulls a pawn out of a fight (it pauses while drafted).
[*]The pawn fixes their [b]own[/b] carried weapon and keeps it equipped the whole time, no juggling spare weapons.
[*][b]Need it right now?[/b] Select a pawn, right-click a machining table, and choose [b]"Repair (weapon) now"[/b] to force an immediate repair, no matter how worn the weapon is.
[/list]

[h1]Always know what a repair needs[/h1]
[list]
[*]Select a damaged weapon or piece of gear and its info shows the exact materials a repair needs and how many you currently have.
[*]If a pawn wants to auto-repair its weapon but you are out of the material, you get a quick [b]heads-up message[/b] in the corner naming the pawn and what is missing.
[/list]

[h1]Fully tunable[/h1]
Everything is adjustable in the mod options:
[list]
[*][b]Weapon wear rate[/b] - how fast weapons wear (0 turns wear off completely, 1 is normal, 2 is double).
[*][b]Quality influence[/b] - how much weapon quality matters (0 ignores quality entirely).
[*][b]Repair material cost[/b] - the share of an item's own materials a full repair costs.
[*][b]Auto-repair condition[/b] - how worn a carried weapon gets before pawns fix it on their own (0 turns it off).
[/list]

[h1]Compatibility[/h1]
[list]
[*]Requires [b]Harmony[/b].
[*][b]Safe to add[/b] to an existing save at any time.
[*]Removing it mid-save leaves any already-worn weapons at their current condition.
[/list]
